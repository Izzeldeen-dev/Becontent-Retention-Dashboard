Powerful Questioning Framework

‚ùì Breakthrough Questions

  • Vision Questions: "What would success look like in vivid detail?"
  • Possibility Questions: "What would you do if you knew you couldn't fail?"

The GROW Model Deep Dive

G - GOAL

What do you want?

  • Define specific, measurable outcomes
  • Set realistic timeframes
  • Ensure goals are personally meaningful
  • Create visual success pictures

R - REALITY

What's happening now?

  • Assess current situation objectively
  • Identify resources available
  • Recognize obstacles and challenges
  • Explore past attempts and lessons

O - OPTIONS

What could you do?

  • Brainstorm creative solutions
  • Consider alternative approaches
  • Explore best/worst case scenarios
  • Generate at least 5 options

W - WAY FORWARD

What will you do?

  • Choose specific actions
  • Set clear deadlines
  • Plan accountability measures
  • Address potential obstacles

‚è±Ô∏è GROW Timing Guidelines

Goal: 20% | Reality: 30% | Options: 30% | Way Forward: 20%
