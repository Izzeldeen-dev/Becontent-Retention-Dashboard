Technical Crisis Management

🆘 Emergency Response Protocol

Technical failures: Stay calm, implement backup plan, maintain session value

🛠️ Crisis Response Framework

  • Stay Calm: "Technology happens! Let's solve this together."
  • Quick Assessment: "Can you hear me? Can you see me? What's working?"
  • Immediate Backup: Switch to phone call while troubleshooting video
  • Problem-Solve Together: Make client partner in solution, not victim of problem
  • Value Preservation: Continue coaching conversation regardless of tech issues

⚡ Common Issues & Solutions

  • Audio Problems: Switch to chat + phone, restart application
  • Video Freezing: Turn off video, continue with voice only
  • Internet Instability: Move to phone call, reschedule if needed
  • Platform Crashes: Have backup platform ready (Zoom + Teams)
  • Screen Share Issues: Send materials via email/WhatsApp

🎯 Recovery Strategies

  • Humor & Perspective: "Well, that's why we have Plan B!"
  • Extended Session: Add 10-15 minutes to compensate for lost time
  • Follow-up Value: Send extra resources or schedule bonus check-in
  • Learning Opportunity: "How do you usually handle unexpected challenges?"
  • Documentation: Note issues for future prevention

Active Listening Mastery

🎧 The Foundation of Exceptional Coaching

  • Full Presence: Eliminate all distractions - phones away, notifications off, complete focus
  • The 3-Second Rule: Wait 3 seconds after client finishes speaking before responding
  • Reflective Listening: "What I'm hearing is..." "It sounds like you're saying..."
  • Emotional Awareness: Notice tone, energy shifts, and what's NOT being said
  • Clarifying Questions: "Help me understand..." "Can you tell me more about..."

🔍 Advanced Listening Techniques

  • Micro-Expression Reading: Watch for facial cues, even in video calls
  • Energy Matching: Mirror their communication pace and emotional state
  • Silence as a Tool: Use strategic pauses to encourage deeper sharing
  • Summarizing Skills: Synthesize key points every 5-7 minutes
  • Memory Techniques: Reference previous conversations to show continuity

Powerful Questioning Framework

❓ Breakthrough Questions

  • Vision Questions: "What would success look like in vivid detail?"
  • Possibility Questions: "What would you do if you knew you couldn't fail?"
  • Learning Questions: "What has this experience taught you about yourself?"
  • Action Questions: "What's the smallest step you could take today?"
  • Values Questions: "What's most important to you about this goal?"

🚫 Questions to Avoid

  • Leading Questions: "Don't you think you should..."
  • Multiple Questions: Asking 3 questions at once
  • Why Questions: Can create defensiveness
  • Closed Questions: Yes/no answers limit exploration
  • Problem-Focused: "What's wrong with..." or "Why can't you..."

💡 The Question Sequence Method

1. Explore → "Tell me about..." 2. Clarify → "What do you mean by..." 3. Deepen → "What else..." 4. Focus → "What's most important..." 5. Action → "What will you do..."

The GROW Model Deep Dive

G - GOAL

What do you want?

  • Define specific, measurable outcomes
  • Set realistic timeframes
  • Ensure goals are personally meaningful
  • Create visual success pictures

R - REALITY

What's happening now?

  • Assess current situation objectively
  • Identify resources available
  • Recognize obstacles and challenges
  • Explore past attempts and lessons

O - OPTIONS

What could you do?

  • Brainstorm creative solutions
  • Consider alternative approaches
  • Explore best/worst case scenarios
  • Generate at least 5 options

W - WAY FORWARD

What will you do?

  • Choose specific actions
  • Set clear deadlines
  • Plan accountability measures
  • Address potential obstacles

⏱️ GROW Timing Guidelines

Goal: 20% | Reality: 30% | Options: 30% | Way Forward: 20%
